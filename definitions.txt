Tylenol: Tylenol is commonly used to relieve pain and reduce fever. It has been used for decades and is safe when taken as directed.
Ibuprofen: Ibuprofen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug that helps reduce pain, swelling, and fever.
CoughSyrup: Cough syrup helps suppress coughing and soothe throat irritation.
Antihistamine: Antihistamines are used to relieve allergy symptoms such as sneezing, itching, and runny nose.
